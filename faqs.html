FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Frequently asked faq's

Below are some frequently asked FAQs by our customers.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept major credit/debit cards, PayPal, and other digital payment options.

Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we provide international shipping. Shipping fees and delivery times depend on your location.

Can I return or exchange a product?

You can return or exchange products within 30 days of purchase, as long as they are in their original condition with all accessories included.

What warranty do you offer on VR accessories?

All VR accessories come with a 1-year manufacturer's warranty, covering any hardware defects.

Which gaming consoles do you sell?

We offer the latest models of PlayStation, Xbox, and Nintendo consoles.

Are your VR accessories compatible with all VR headsets?

Most of our VR accessories are compatible with major VR headsets like Oculus, HTC Vive, and PlayStation VR. Check the product descriptions for specific compatibility.

How can I track my order?

After your order ships, you’ll receive a tracking number via email. You can use it to track your order on our website or the courier’s site.
